Financial Decision Dashboard

Advanced analytics & visualization platform for market insights and portfolio strategies

Purpose

This dashboard is designed to support investment decision-making by providing real-time market data, risk metrics, and portfolio optimization tools. Built with Python, Streamlit, and financial APIs, it showcases quantitative finance, data science, and visualization skills.

Features

  • Live market data retrieval and visualization
  • Key performance indicators: annualized return, volatility, Sharpe ratio
  • Risk analysis: Value at Risk (VaR) and drawdowns
  • Portfolio optimization (Markowitz mean-variance)
  • Monte Carlo simulations for price projections
  • Machine learning signal generation and backtesting
  • Exportable reports in CSV and PDF
